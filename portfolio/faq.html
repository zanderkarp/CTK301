Frequently Asked Questions

Is Zander short for something, like Alexander?

No.

What do you use to make your art?

Photoshop.

What inspires you to make your art?

I usually just have an idea and go for it. There's no wrong answers in art...at least that's what my mom tells me. And I never doubt my mom.

What is your art process?

I sketch out my ideas like a kindergartener just picked up a pencil for the first time. After that, I go section by section and repeat to myself "Trust the process" while it looks like a drawing your kid made for the fridge until it looks amazing. Works every time.

What's with the different color eye?

It's the evil eye. I have a necklace of it and it's my favorite. Like an "I don't care if the colors don't match my outfit today" kind of favorite. So every time I draw myself, I give myself an evil eye.

Why do you never draw your glasses in your self portraits?

I forget to every single time.

Do you have any advice for any aspiring artists?

If you find a coin on the floor, only pick it up if it's heads side up. If it's tails side up, don't do it, it's not worth it.
